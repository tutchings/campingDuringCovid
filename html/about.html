About Us

This application provides a search for National Parks based on proximity to a city, state, zip code, or address. A map is rendered that provides markers for each park. Data is provided above the map in bright colors warning users of the current trend in Covid-19 transmission based on the percentage increase over the past 7 days in positive cases, hospitalizations, and deaths. This information is provided by the Covid Tracking Project. This application is for information purposes only.

It was developed by Alex Nordin, Benjamin Smith, Reed Taylor, and Thomas Tutchings. The purpose of this website is for educational purposes only. We are in no way affiliated with the U.S. National Parks Service. Please feel free to contact us!
